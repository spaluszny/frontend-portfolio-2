# Audio Engineering and Sound Production Portfolio
<br/>

## BY SARAH PALUSZNY
<br/>

<hr/>
<br/>

When I attended the Jacobs School of Music at IU, I spent a lot of time in the recording studio. I wanted to showcase this in an online portfolio that provided a cool retro style. My time at IU was spent into three different main categories for recording: live sound, studio, and post production. 
<br/>
Three albums were made for those corresponding categories in Photoshop. Then with simple CSS hover techniques I was able to create a feeling of opening the albums to look at. 
<br/>
To showcase my studio work I wanted a fun interactive way to play the song. I was able to find a tutorial on youtube the makes a recorder player that you can press play and then listen to a song. This then inspired me to do something creative for my video in the post production. With CSS, I was able to create a retro looking tv that looks similar to the one on the post production album. 
<br/>
When I finished my website, I ran a speed test on it and although I was happy with the look, I found the performace, accessibility, best practices, and SEO could all be improved. My images were too big and I wasn't including enough metadata for the SEO.
